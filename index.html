Mixed Waste → Fuel Conversion System

A single-reactor hydrothermal platform converting mixed municipal waste into fuels and valuable chemical feedstocks.

Global Waste Problem

Food Waste

Organic waste decomposes and releases methane in landfills.

Textile Waste

Polyester and cellulose textiles are difficult to recycle.

Plastic Waste

Contaminated plastics are rejected from recycling plants.

Proposed Technology

Our reactor directly converts mixed waste streams into fuels using hydrothermal conversion, eliminating sorting and drying steps.

No Sorting

Mixed waste is processed directly.

No Drying

Wet waste enhances reaction chemistry.

Energy Recovery

Heat exchangers recycle process heat.
