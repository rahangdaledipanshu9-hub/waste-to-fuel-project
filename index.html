Mixed Waste Catalytic Pyrolysis Reactor

Project Overview

This project demonstrates catalytic co-pyrolysis of plastic, textile and biomass waste to produce hydrocarbon fuel.

Reactor Design

The reactor operates at 420-430°C with activated carbon and CaO catalyst.

Experimental Results

  • Oil Yield: ~70%
  • Gas Yield: ~20%
  • Char Yield: ~10%

Characterization

FTIR analysis confirmed hydrocarbon compounds with peaks at 2922 cm⁻¹ and 2853 cm⁻¹.

Future Scope

This technology can be scaled for decentralized waste-to-energy systems.
