# Hydrothermal Mixed Waste Conversion Reactor

A conceptual engineering project proposing a **hydrothermal reactor system** capable of converting mixed municipal waste streams (food waste, plastics, and textiles) into **usable fuels and chemical feedstocks**.
The system aims to reduce landfill dependency while recovering energy from waste materials.

---

## Project Overview

Municipal solid waste management is a growing global problem.
Large amounts of organic waste, plastics, and textile materials are disposed of in landfills, leading to:

* Environmental pollution
* Methane emissions
* Loss of potentially recoverable energy resources

Traditional recycling systems require **extensive sorting and drying**, which limits their effectiveness when dealing with mixed waste streams.

This project proposes a **single-reactor hydrothermal conversion system** capable of processing mixed waste directly and converting it into useful chemical and energy products.

---

## Problem Statement

Modern waste management systems face several challenges:

* Mixed waste streams that are difficult to separate
* High moisture content in organic waste
* Limited recycling capability for contaminated plastics
* Increasing landfill usage

These issues result in inefficient resource utilization and environmental harm.

---

## Proposed Solution

The proposed system uses **hydrothermal processing** to convert mixed waste streams into valuable outputs.

Hydrothermal processes use **high temperature and pressure water environments** to break down complex organic materials into simpler chemical compounds.

The system can process:

* Food waste
* Plastic waste
* Textile waste

without requiring extensive preprocessing.

---

## System Architecture

Waste is processed through the following stages:

1. Waste Input
2. Pre-treatment (optional shredding or size reduction)
3. Hydrothermal Reactor
4. Thermal and Chemical Decomposition
5. Product Separation
6. Fuel and Chemical Output

This pipeline enables the conversion of mixed waste into usable products.

---

## Key Features

* Ability to process **mixed waste streams**
* Reduced need for waste sorting
* Compatible with **wet organic waste**
* Potential for **energy recovery**
* Reduction of landfill waste
* Conversion of waste into **useful fuels and chemicals**
